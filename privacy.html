Privacy Policy

Soon — Last updated June 18, 2026

Soon is built to respect your privacy. The short version: your data stays on your device, and we never collect it.

What we collect

Nothing. Soon has no account system, no servers, and no analytics. Your data is stored only on your device (and in your private iCloud backup, if enabled). It never leaves your phone and is never sent to us or to any third party.

Tracking

Soon does not track you. There are no advertising identifiers, no third-party SDKs, and no usage analytics.

Notifications

Soon schedules reminders locally on your device. Notifications are created and delivered entirely on your phone; nothing is sent to a server.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information.

Children

Soon does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
